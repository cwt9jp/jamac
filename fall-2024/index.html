Fall JAMAC 2024

This year, we are hosting a Fall JAMAC, with more opportunities for mathletes to hone their skills before the AMC8/MATHCOUNTS season. For an added twist, there will be many opportunities for mathletes to earn candy throughout the events, alongside the main awards.

Candy Opportunities!

We have two special events for the fall that reward candy instead of medals, for a sweeter twist and lower stakes, both during lunch. We will have a candy guessing game (that will be revealed at the event to keep it competitive), whose winner will be able to take an ample supply of candy home, and a Trivia Round, where contestants will compete to answer a question with four choices the fastest, and whoever does will recieve candy (More rules will be disclosed at the event). Finally, the Quiz Bowl will reward candy for a correct answer and for the top three competitors.
