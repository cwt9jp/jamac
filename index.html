JAMAC 2024-25

The Albemarle Math Honor Society and Math Club are hosting two JAMAC events this year, one in the fall and one in the spring. For more information on these events, please see below.

Fall JAMAC 2024

This year, we are hosting a Fall JAMAC, with more opportunities for mathletes to hone their skills prior to the AMC8/MATHCOUNTS season. For an added twist, there will be many opportunties for mathletes to earn candy throughout the events, alongside the main awards.
